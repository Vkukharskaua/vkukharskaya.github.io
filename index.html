Swoop

Hi! I'm Swoop, Eastern Washington University's mascot, is an ambassador, spirit leader, and fun-loving character for the University. A popular character among EWU fans, alumni, & students, he’s adored by all walks of life. His charm, attitude, and high-spirited personality combine to make up why he’s America’s Favorite Mascot.
